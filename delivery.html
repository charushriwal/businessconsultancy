Delivery

Once decisions have been made, it is not always possible for existing company resources to deliver the required strategic projects. Existing resources may not have the skills required to manage projects effectively, or simply don’t have the time to run a project and remain on top of their business as usual work. CS Consulting provides a short to medium term contracting service to address this requirement.

Project Management

Employing project management methodologies, our consulting resources will manage cross-functional project teams in order to deliver your strategic projects. From Project plans to steering committee status updates, we will create a project management structure that includes an appropriate amount of control without stifling agility and flexibility.
