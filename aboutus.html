About Us

CS Consulting offers the comprehensive capabilities and deep industry knowledge necessary to help you navigate the complexity of development. We have experience in developing different types of environment in multiple areas, and in all stages of development, from translational research through post marketing studies.

All team members are board certified with additional credentials and fellowship training. We have helped many companies achieve their goals over the years. We bring a unique blend of strategic thinking and over 15 years of experience to help sponsors tackle the complexity of business development.

Our team is made up of business strategists, accountants, entrepreneurs, restructuring specialists and business rescue practitioners, with a network of additional resources to call on as required. Each engagement is unique and we will tailor an approach and create a plan that is specific to your business and will help it become stable, sustainable and profitable.

So, at CS Consulting we believe that every business deserves a chance. Our primary motivation in all business proceedings is to turn the business around, and as such we focus as much on the long-term strategic plan as we do the immediate actions required to effect proceedings and avoid liquidation. We support business and people across a number of areas. Our approach is firmly based on honesty, transparency and being straightforward.

Our philosophy is to build a practice head and shoulders above the run of the mill insolvency practices. This is achieved by:

  • Putting clients at the heart of our business – a practice clients can depend on
  • Taking an honest, transparent and straight forward approach
  • Allaying the fear and anxiety around deciding to contact an Insolvency Practitioner
  • Giving excellent client service from highly trained and sincere staff

All corporate and personal insolvency work is undertaken at our office in Gurugram. We have three Licensed Insolvency Practitioners, all with many years’ experience in dealing with individual, partnership and company debt problems, together with a team of specialist administrators and support staff.

Through our business advice services we help companies gain access to finance, develop their business strategy and help them grow. We help both businesses who are experiencing difficulties and come to us to help them avoid insolvency, turnaround and move forward, as well as successful business who have ambitions to grow further.

We believe that combining the accessibility and experience of our Insolvency Practitioners with staff who work with rather than against you, is what sets CS Consulting apart from our competitors.
